About Us

WELCOME TO ASA FILM CREW

ASA FILM CREW

Serving the Entertainment Industry at Affordable Prices since 1988

ASA has been providing professional licensed and specialized union and non-union crew to the entertainment industry since 1988 at affordable prices.

ASA is the first in the industry to provide you with these services.

ASA is a "one call does it all" operation where production staff can call for budget quotes and book various specialized crew in the areas of Medical and Safety Services, Security Services, and Child Services.

We are also available for various forms needed for using children in production as well as consulting with production on scheduling when children are involved for maximum use of their services within the guidelines of the California Child Labor Laws.

We also have EMT's throughout the U.S. to facilitate your "local hire" production needs to keep production costs low.

ASA has been around for many years to serve you so please do not hesitate to call anytime for your production needs and bookings.
